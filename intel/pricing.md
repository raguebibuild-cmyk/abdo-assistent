## Pricing Model

**Hybrid: Setup Fee + Monthly Retainer**

- Setup fee covers discovery, build, and delivery of the workflow
- Monthly retainer covers maintenance, optimisation, and support
- Annual option available at 15% discount (equivalent to ~2 months free)

This model fits MENA SMEs because:
- The setup fee signals professionalism and commitment
- The retainer creates predictable income without requiring constant new clients
- SMEs understand project fees + maintenance — it mirrors how they hire contractors

---

## Value Metric

**Primary:** Hours saved per month for the client  
**Secondary:** Revenue-generating workflows (CRM, lead follow-up)  
**Pricing basis:** Value delivered, not time spent

**Floor:** Always above cost of the next best alternative (hiring a part-time admin or subscribing to 3+ SaaS tools combined)

## Annual Discount

All monthly retainers: **15% off when paid annually upfront** (equivalent to ~2 months free).

Present this as an option during quoting — don't lead with it.

---

## First-Client Strategy

For the first 1-2 clients: offer a **20% discount in exchange for a written testimonial and permission to use as a case study**. Frame it as a founding client rate — not a discount.

Do not offer this beyond the first 2 clients.

---

## Positioning

**We are not the cheapest option.** We are positioned between:
- DIY tools (Zapier, Make) — which require technical skill and ongoing management
- Hiring full-time staff — which is 5-10x more expensive

Our price reflects the fact that we build it, run it, and maintain it.

## Quoting Rules

- Never quote without confirming which package fits the client's needs first
- Always include both setup and monthly in the quote — never just one
- Pricing is always in euros (€) unless the client explicitly requests local currency
- Flag any service not listed here — confirm pricing before quoting
