# Gold Standard — Client Delivery Email

**What this demonstrates:** Professional but warm tone. No jargon. Short. Clear next step.
**Use this as the benchmark for all client-facing communication.**

---

**Subject: Your workflow is ready for review**

Hi [Name],

Good news — I've finished building the first version of your client onboarding workflow.

It handles intake forms, sends confirmation emails automatically, and drops new client data straight into your sheet. No manual copy-paste.

I'd love to walk you through it this week. Does Thursday or Friday work for a 20-minute call?

Abderrahim
degiabdo

---

**Why this works:**
- Opens with the result, not the process
- Explains the benefit in plain language (no jargon)
- One clear ask at the end
- Warm but not over-familiar
