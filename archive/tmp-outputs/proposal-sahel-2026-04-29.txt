PAGE 2 — EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

Context

Sahel Cafe Group operates 8 locations across the UAE, managing complex day-to-day
operations including ordering, inventory, customer engagement, and staff coordination.
As the group scales, manual processes create friction, slow decision-making, and
introduce inconsistencies across locations.

Objective

Arabic AI Agents proposes deploying 3 custom automation workflows under the Growth
Package to streamline Sahel Cafe Group's core operations. The goal: reduce manual
workload, improve consistency across all 8 locations, and free leadership to focus
on growth rather than operations.

Our Approach

We build, deploy, document, and train your team — then stay close for 60 days
post-launch to ensure the workflows hold. No generic templates. Every workflow is
mapped to your actual processes and tools.
